About

We are a community dedicated to restore the classical Counter-Strike 1.6 gameplay. Started by haZh back in the year 2019 January, our server has known by gamers around the world that took us to the point we are at today. Even though this wasn't a big hit like any other server that you may know, we hope to continue doing what we love doing for the most part.

  • Classic maps
  • Custom sprays
  • Colored lights, smoke and hud
  • Server side anti cheat
  • Polite team of staff

Rules

In order to keep each and everything under control as fair as possible, we enforce anyone and everyone who join our server to follow these easy rules below. If you see anyone, including any staff member that isn't following these rules, you can either join our chat or send us an immediate email for further action.

  • No bullying
  • No spamming
  • No cheating
  • No hacking
